## About

I am an AI Engineer with a mechatronics engineering background, building full-stack AI applications, RAG platforms, LLMOps workflows, and agentic systems. I am also pursuing an M.Sc. in Mechatronics Engineering at Isparta University of Applied Sciences.

My work sits at the intersection of real-world engineering and applied AI: inverter testing, BMS diagnostics, energy systems, hardware logs, semantic search, tool-using agents, and production-ready observability.
